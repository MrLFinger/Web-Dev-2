The FIFA Club World Cup is an international men's football tournament contested by the champion clubs from each of the six continental confederations, plus the champion club from the host nation.

The Club World Cup will see 32 teams divided into eight groups of four teams. Teams are all selected from around the world if they reach the qualifications. The teams in each group play each other in round-robin and the top two teams progress to the knockout stage of the competition, or the last 16. Then from here are teams who have past the group stages now elimating a team each match until the finals.
